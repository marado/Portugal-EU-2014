= European Treaties =

A single member state should be able to block a treaty change, even if all the other members states agree to it?

These parties agree:
 * BE
 * PCTP/MRPP
 * CDU

These parties neither agree or disagree, or have no oppinion:
 * PAN
 * MPT

These parties disagree:
 * Livre
 * PS
 * AP

Source: [4]
